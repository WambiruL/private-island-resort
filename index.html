About Mazuri Private Island Resort

Located in Mombasa, Mazuri Private Island Resort is a tranquil retreat for travellers on business or leisure, offering the ideal place to break the journey for those passing through Mombasa Town. The hotel is located off the main Mombasa-Nairobi highway just outside of Mombasa town and is situated on a wooded avenue in the upscale Milimani area adjacent to the State House.
