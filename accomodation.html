All rooms at Mazuri Private Island Resort Accommodation are ensuite and feature contemporary guest facilities and services to ensure a tranquil, comfortable and pleasant stay. The amenities include, air-conditioning, black-out curtains, Satellite TV, tea/coffee facilities, mini-bar, safe-deposit box, direct dial telephone with VOIP, wireless internet connectivity, private bath, shower and WC, hair dryers (voltage of 240 volts) and 50 cycle razor outlets operating on both 220 and 240 volts.
